ABOUT INDIA ITME SOCIETY

India International Textile Machinery Exhibitions Society (India ITME Society), a 45 year old non profit apex industry body, established in 1980, to support and serve the Textile Engineering Industry through Exhibitions & Events.

India ITME Society was conceived with a Vision to play a pivotal role in strengthening the Indian Textile Engineering Industry by facilitating exchange of knowledge, technology transfer & foreign investment in the sector and to be the apex exhibition body in India for the textile machinery, accessories and component industry.

The organisation and its team works relentlessly towards its Mission to aid, stimulate, promote, encourage & support trade and commerce with a focus on Textile Engineering and allied industries, through exhibitions and conferences, thus propagating the cause of Textile Engineering Industry at National and International levels.

With the motto of empowering Textiles through Trade & Technology, India ITME Society relentlessly spots the right opportunities that will contribute to the greater good. Over the years India ITME Society has evolved into the next level, metamorphosing from an Indian organiser to a global phenomenon embracing internationalism exploring & connecting emerging economies to Indian Textile & Engineering Industry.
