Knowledge sessions

Knowledge is Everything

There is no greater gift than the gift of knowledge. In our path of bringing international textile machinery manufacturers to India through our exhibitions we are constantly introduced with latest innovations & learning’s across cross countries. The pace with which new developments & technologies are introduced it is important to have a dedicated calendar book that ensures the knowledge is dissipated to the industry as well as to the institutes and young minds. This knowledge session will provide following services.

The conference / Seminars/ Panel Sessions/ training programs will be a connect with global experts on the subject enabling industry/student/faculty to gain the latest information on the cutting-edge technology, AI in manufacturing, sustainability, digitization and the revolving dynamics of textile engineering and technology manufacturing.

ITME is always on the verge of bringing more and more for the betterment of the textile ecosystem and overall growth of the textile industry
